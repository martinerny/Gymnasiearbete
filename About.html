Who Am I?

I’m a 18-year-old student living in Sweden, with a big passion for tech and a love for learning new things and solving problems in creative ways.
When I’m not studying or messing around with something new, I like being outdoors—as long as the weather’s decent. I’m not built for the cold, so I definitely prefer sunny, warmer days. On those days, you’ll probably find me outside being active, playing DiscGolf, which is one of my hobbies.

Education

I’m currently studying Data at Elprogrammet, Polhemsskolan, since 2022 and is set to graduate in summer 2025. Over this time, I’ve been building up my skills in areas like networking and programming—both challenging and genuinely fun.
I chose to study Data because it’s something I’m really interested in, and I want to understand more about tech in general. It’s such a big part of the future, and I’d love to be a part of shaping where it goes.
